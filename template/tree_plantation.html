Successful Tree Plantation Drive in Mumbai

Posted on August 16, 2025

We’re proud to share that our recent tree plantation drive in Mumbai was a big success, with over 500 trees planted thanks to the amazing support from local volunteers. People from all walks of life came together to make our city greener and healthier.

These trees will help improve air quality, reduce heat, and support local wildlife. But planting is just the start—we’re committed to caring for these trees so they grow strong and thrive in the years ahead.

Green Horizon is excited to continue this momentum with more drives and awareness programs across Mumbai. Join us in making a lasting impact—because together, we can build a greener future.
