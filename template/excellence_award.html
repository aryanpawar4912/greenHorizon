Environmental Excellence Award: Honoring Our Commitment to a Greener Tomorrow

Posted on August 16, 2025

Green Horizon is proud to have been honored with the Environmental Excellence Award in recognition of our impactful work in promoting urban sustainability. This award celebrates the hard work, dedication, and passion of our team and volunteers who continue to drive change in our cities.

Over the years, we’ve worked closely with communities to plant trees, conserve water, reduce waste, and raise awareness about the importance of protecting our environment. Each initiative, big or small, has contributed to building more sustainable, resilient urban spaces.

This recognition motivates us to keep pushing forward and expanding our efforts. We invite you to read more about our journey and be a part of the movement for a cleaner, greener future.
