About Green Horizon

Green Horizon is a dedicated environmental NGO working towards a sustainable future. Our mission is to protect and preserve nature through community engagement, tree plantation drives, and environmental education programs.

We aim to create green spaces, promote eco-friendly practices, and empower local communities to take action against climate change. With a network of volunteers across India, we organize clean-up campaigns, conservation projects, and awareness initiatives to foster environmental stewardship.
